<p><ul><li>Fully Demineralized</li><li>Incomplete Demineralized</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Sweet Whey Powder market is divided into two main types: Fully Demineralized (FDM) and Incomplete Demineralized (IDM). Fully Demineralized whey powder has had most of its mineral content removed, making it suitable for applications requiring low mineral levels, such as infant formulas and specialty nutrition products. Incomplete Demineralized whey powder retains some mineral content, making it ideal for general food applications, dairy products, and nutrition supplements where a balance of nutrients is desired. Both types cater to specific consumer needs and industry requirements.</p></p>
