<p><strong>The Perilla Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Toasted Perilla Oil</li><li>Untoasted Perilla Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Perilla oil is predominantly classified into two market types: toasted and untoasted perilla oil. Toasted perilla oil is produced by roasting the seeds before extraction, resulting in a rich, nutty flavor and dark color, making it popular for culinary applications like salad dressings and marinades. Untoasted perilla oil, on the other hand, is extracted from raw seeds, offering a lighter flavor and pale color, often used for health benefits and as a cooking oil in various dishes. Both types cater to distinct consumer preferences in the food and wellness markets.</p></p>
