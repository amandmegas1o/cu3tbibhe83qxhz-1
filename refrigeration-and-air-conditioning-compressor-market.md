<p><p>The Refrigeration and Air Conditioning Compressor market includes various types, notably Rotary-Screw Compressors and Reciprocating Compressors. Rotary-Screw Compressors use two interlocking screws to compress refrigerant, offering high efficiency and continuous operation, making them ideal for large-scale applications. In contrast, Reciprocating Compressors utilize a piston mechanism to compress gas, providing a more compact solution suitable for residential or small commercial use. Both types play crucial roles in HVAC systems, catering to diverse cooling needs across various sectors.</p></p>
