<p><p>The fingerprint biometrics machine market is divided into two main types: single finger and multi-finger systems. Single finger scanners capture the print from one finger at a time, making them cost-effective and widely used in various applications like mobile devices and entry systems. Multi-finger scanners, on the other hand, can capture multiple fingerprints simultaneously, providing enhanced security and accuracy, making them ideal for high-security environments such as border control, law enforcement, and secure access areas.</p></p>
